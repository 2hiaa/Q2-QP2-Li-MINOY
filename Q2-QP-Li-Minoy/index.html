Akap is a youth-led organization that collaborates with multiple orphanages in Davao City, aiming to establish a healthy and nurturing environment for their children by providing donations and necessary supplies.

Since its inception, AKAP, a dynamic youth-led organization, has made remarkable strides in creating positive change within multiple orphanages in Davao City. With a passionate commitment to fostering a healthy and nurturing environment for children in need, AKAP has successfully collaborated with various orphanages to provide essential donations and supplies. Through dedicated efforts and community engagement, the organization has significantly enhanced the living conditions and well-being of the children under its care. AKAP's impactful initiatives have included the provision of educational resources, healthcare essentials, and recreational activities, all aimed at creating a supportive atmosphere for the children to thrive. The organization's unwavering dedication to uplifting the lives of these young individuals has not only left a lasting impact on the orphanages but has also ignited a sense of hope and resilience within the hearts of the children it serves. As AKAP continues to grow and evolve, its mission remains clear: to build a brighter future for the youth in Davao City's orphanages, one compassionate act at a time.
